groovn' n' movin'

Keep the rythm and dance movement going, 'cause we're gonna be GROOVN' N' MOVIN'! We've brought back everyone's facorite arcade game, now accessible in a polished and easy to play mode. Play our web game with either our homebrew pad or your own handy-dandy keyboard.

Plug in the board USB, and wait a couple of minutes, then hit the PLAY button below. If there is no USB device, you can still play the game with the arrow keys on your keyboard.

Game Instructions: Just STEP on four arrowed pads on the board to match the arrows that scroll up the screen in time with a chosen song
